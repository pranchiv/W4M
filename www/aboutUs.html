Wheels 4 Meals was started as an Eagle Scout project by Boy Scout Pranav Chivukula, a senior at Pennsbury High School. With help from a mentor, he was able to bring this website into existence, in the hopes that it will make a difference in providing food to those who need it the most. It is currently operated in certain areas of Bucks County, but Pranav hopes that it will be able to impact all who face this issue.
